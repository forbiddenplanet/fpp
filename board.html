All the Pretty Faces!

Our board is involved. Like, so involved we wonder how they don't have tentacles.

Board is like the permanent members of FPP. You don't have to join board to be part of the FPPhamily, and in each show, the actors, stage crew, and production team don't need to be board members. Instead, board handles some of the more administrative and behind-the-scenes tasks and assists with tech weeks, getting that stuff out of the way so the process for each show can be extra smooth. Just the way we like it. No offense to people who prefer chunky.

If being a permanent part of FPP sounds like fun (it is, we promise!) or you have any questions about what board members do, feel free to shoot us an email or slide into our DMs. And while you're here, check out those pretty faces!
