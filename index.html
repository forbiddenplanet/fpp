    • Welcome to Forbidden Planet.

    Where there's three shows a semester, a dedicated bunch of people, and a whole lotta fun.

    Since you're here, we'll give you a bit of background. We're one of four student theatre groups at GW, and we do mostly musicals with the occasional straight play. We're famous for putting on the Rocky Horror Picture Show every Halloween- it's the largest theatrical event on campus! We also do a musical cabaret each spring, with our two other shows each semester directed by different members of the GW theatre community. We're here to have fun and put on a great show, and you'll find that's heavily reflected in the degree of effort put forth by every member of our company.

    Be sure to check out the pretty faces that make up our board of producers, and scroll down to see this semester's season. And, of course, if having fun sounds like fun, get involved! You're gonna have a good time- that's our FPPromise.

    The 13th Annual Musical Cabaret

    Get lucky and come see this semester's musical extravaganza! This ten song showcase will have songs from Hamilton, Little Shop of Horrors, The Greatest Showman, Fiddler on the Roof, and more- arriving February 28th, March 1st, and March 2nd at the Betts Theatre in the Marvin Center. Be sure to sign up for our email list to keep an eye out for tryout dates, coming soon!

    A New Brain

    Get a head start on finals and come see our mandatory artsy show of the semester, directed by Jordan Askey. It'll be on display March 28th, 29th, and 30th in the West Hall Theatre. Stay tuned for tryout dates! Please note, brains are not actually provided at the show so be sure to bring your own.

    Mamma Mia!
