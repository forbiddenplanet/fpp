Welcome to Forbidden Planet.

Where there's three shows a semester, a dedicated bunch of people, and a whole lotta fun.

Since you're here, we'll give you a bit of background. We're one of four student theatre groups at GW, and we do mostly musicals with the occasional straight play. We're famous for putting on the Rocky Horror Picture Show every Halloween- it's the largest theatrical event on campus! We also do a musical cabaret each spring, with our two other shows each semester directed by different members of the GW theatre community. We're here to have fun and put on a great show, and you'll find that's heavily reflected in the degree of effort put forth by every member of our company.

Be sure to check out the pretty faces that make up our board of producers, and scroll down to see this semester's season. And, of course, if having fun sounds like fun, feel free to get involved! You're gonna have a good time- that's our FPPromise.
